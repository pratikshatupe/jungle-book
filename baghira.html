BAGIRA

INFORMATION OF BAGIRA

Bagheera is a fictional character in Rudyard Kipling's Mowgli stories in The Jungle Book and The Second Jungle Book. He is a black panther who serves as friend, protector and mentor to the "man-cub" Mowgli.
Species: Black panther
Creator: Rudyard Kipling
First appearance: "Mowgli's Brothers"
Last appearance: "The Spring Running"
